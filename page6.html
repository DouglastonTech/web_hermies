Hermit crabs have a small and very soft tail. It must be protected inside of the shell. When their shell home gets too small or dirty, they will go searching for a new one. At that time, you should provide them with a few new shells. If you are not sure when they need shells, have empty shells always available in their cage, approximately 10-15% bigger than ones they had. If you put the shells in the cage, they will move into them by themselves when they are ready. It can also be a great toy for them , even if hermit crabs don't move to new shells right away.
