Land hermit crabs are interesting creatures. Hermit crabs are great pets because they do not carry known diseases. They are hypo-allergenic, which makes hermit crabs a perfect pet for those with allergies.

Hermit crabs are very low maintenance and are easy to take care of. Also, they are odorless. In the wild lands, hermit crabs live inland away from water. Hermit crabs are nocturnal and are more active at night than during the day. Some natives even refer to them as soldier crabs due to their habit of trooping in numbers.
